This is a mentorship booking Platform for aspiring and early Entreprenuers with less than 2 years in business. Research has identified that more than 95% of businesses collapse after 5 years. Additionaly research as also shown that, Entreprenuers who had access to mentorship had two times more potential to succed than those without mentorship.

This platform provides the opportunity for aspiring Entreprenuers and young Entreprenuers to get access to business mentors that will see their businesses succeed and survive beyond the 5 years mark and even become IPOs.

Reasons to consider using our service

  • We provide you access to more than 100 different mentors
  • You will have access to different mentors who have experience in different business fields and business departments
  • Payment with cryptocurrency. We built on top of Ethereum blockchain to ensure everyone can easily pay for sessions.
  • Transparency. With blockchain, we can't be able to delete your session payment. Moreover, you will also have access to your booked sessions permanently.
  • Affordability. Our mentorship sessions are made affordable for everyone, as we aim to empower many Entrepreneurs who will create more jobs and build the economy.
